About ReWear

Learn more about our mission to create a sustainable clothing community

Our Story

ReWear was born from a simple idea: what if we could make it easier for people to give their clothes a second life while helping those in need? We believe that every piece of clothing has value, and by connecting donors with receivers, we're not just reducing waste—we're building a more caring community.

Reduce Waste

Help keep clothes out of landfills and reduce textile waste in our communities.

Help Others

Provide quality clothing to those who need it most, fostering generosity and support.

Build Community

Connect neighbors and create meaningful relationships through shared values.
