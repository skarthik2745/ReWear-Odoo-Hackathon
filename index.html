Our Mission

Every day, thousands of wearable clothes are wasted, while many people still lack proper clothing. ReWear aims to connect donors and receivers through a transparent, feedback-driven web platform.

Key Features

User Roles

Donor, Receiver, and Admin roles for full functionality and community management

Donate & Receive

Upload clothes and request items with just a few clicks. Easy photo upload and item details

Feedback Points

Receivers rate donors with up to 50 points per donation. Build trust through honest feedback

Leaderboard

Top donors ranked by community feedback. Monthly, weekly, and all-time rankings

Badge System

Earn badges for milestones like first donation, high ratings, and community contributions

Mobile Friendly

Fully responsive design that works perfectly on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile
